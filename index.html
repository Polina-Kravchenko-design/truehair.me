How does True Hair® work?

Over 7-10 days, your hair gradually regains its original shade, elasticity, shine, and strength, allowing a subtle and natural transformation that blends seamlessly with your appearance.

The color of our hair is determined by melanin, a pigment found in the hair's internal structure. As we age, the body reduces or stops delivering melanin to the hair, leading to gray or white strands.

Unlike traditional hair dyes, which coat the surface, True Hair® works from within. Its active ingredients restore the natural color by reducing oxidized, colorless melanin.

How to use True Hair®?

Apply a small amount of True Hair® evenly to slightly damp hair, ensuring it is just lightly misted after application.

Use a comb to gently work the product evenly through your hair.

After application, let the hair dry freely or blow dry with a hair dryer.

Firstly, use the product three to four times a week, preferably in the morning. Visible results typically begin within the first week as your hair gradually returns to its natural color. Once the desired effect is achieved, reduce usage to once or twice a week to maintain the results.
